About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a professional engineer that has worked with aeronautical PLC and pneumatic equipment, digital electronics and microcontrollers, PCB design, CAD, software and hardware programming, manufacturing and machining design, as well as web development. I have experience designing products from concept to industrialisation, full life-cycle design and project management.

I completed my Bachelor's degree from Vanderbilt in 2012. I took leadership roles such as a FIRST Robotics instructor, and was awarded 1st place for my senior design thesis.

Personally, I am a lover of engineering and science, and spend my free time on side projects, learning new skills, and getting into new hobbies. From the depths of marine science and diving, to the skies offered by being a private pilot, I am interested in the wide range of what the world has to offer!

This website serves as an accessible CV and provides an overview of some of my professional and personal projects and interests.
